# Business Objectives

## Define User Roles Clearly
Establish clear responsibilities for Alice (Project Manager) and Bob (Team Member).

## Implement Access Control Mechanisms
Restrict Bob from creating or modifying project records while allowing updates to assigned tasks.

## Streamline Workflow Processes
Enable Alice to assign tasks and monitor completion efficiently.

## Improve Accountability
Ensure task ownership and progress tracking are transparent.

## Enhance Collaboration
Provide notifications and structured workflows.

## Increase Operational Efficiency
Automate task assignments and updates.

## Ensure Security
Apply ACLs and role-based restrictions to protect project data.
